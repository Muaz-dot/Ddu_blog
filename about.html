About DDU blog

The DDU Blog is a website created for educational purposes while we were learning software tools and practice course. It serves as a platform where the Dire Dawa University community, including students, teachers, researchers, technical experts, and management, can share their experiences, insights, and knowledge. Although the creation of a blog requires membership in the DDU community, the website is globally accessible to anyone.
